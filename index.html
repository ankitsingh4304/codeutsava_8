About Us

Codeutsava is an annual hackathon event organized to bring together the brightest minds in technology and innovation. Our mission is to foster creativity, collaboration, and coding skills through a series of challenges and workshops.


Students from all over India can take part in this event, attend Workshops, participate in the Hackathon, and various other coding related activities which are organized under this event.
